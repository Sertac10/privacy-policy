Privacy Policy

App: PhotoRegen AI
Developer: Sertaç Nayman
Effective Date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PhotoRegen AI handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

PhotoRegen AI does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to the developer’s servers in the current version of the app.

Images selected or created within the app are processed locally on your device.

2. How Images Are Processed

PhotoRegen AI processes images directly on the device. Your photos and edited results are not uploaded to the developer’s servers.

3. Permissions Used by the App

The app may request the following permissions in order to provide its features:

  • Photo / gallery access
  • Camera access
  • Storage / media access
  • Internet access

These permissions are used only to enable app functionality such as selecting images, capturing photos, saving outputs, downloading optional model assets, and showing notifications if applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play asset delivery functionality to provide optional downloadable model files when needed.

At this time, the app does not use advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs for user tracking.

5. Data Storage

User data is not stored on the developer’s servers. Any images or outputs you save remain on your device unless you choose to move or share them yourself.

6. Data Deletion

Since user data is not stored on the developer’s servers, no separate account-based deletion request process is required.

7. Children’s Privacy

PhotoRegen AI is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

8. Future Changes

The current version of the app does not include advertising or personal data collection. If future versions add features such as advertising, analytics, or cloud-based processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

9. International Availability

PhotoRegen AI is made available globally. By using the app, you acknowledge that your use of platform services such as Google Play may also be subject to their own terms and privacy policies.
